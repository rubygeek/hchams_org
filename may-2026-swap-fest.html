Hays Caldwell Ham Radio Swap Fest

Date: Saturday, May 16, 2026
Time: 10:00 AM – 1:00 PM
Location: Southwest Christian Church
2116 Lynnbrook Dr
Austin, TX 78748

Join fellow ham radio enthusiasts for a swap fest! Buy, sell, or trade radio equipment, parts, and accessories. Connect with the local amateur radio community, share knowledge, and discover new and used gear.

What to Expect

  • Equipment tables with ham radio gear
  • Networking with local operators
  • Tables provided with 2 chairs per table

Seller spots are limited — reserve yours early. Each seller receives a table and two chairs at no charge.
