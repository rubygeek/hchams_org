About

Hays-Caldwell Amateur Radio Club, KE5LOT

Promoting Amateur Radio Operation for Emergency Communication, Experimentation, Learning, and Just Fun!

Who We Are

We are a group of licensed “ham operators” and individuals striving to learn the art and get their first license. We provide regular training and assistance to anyone interested in mastering the hobby of radio communication.

Monthly Meetings

We meet on the third Saturday of each month (except December) at the Kyle Public Library at 10:00 AM.
